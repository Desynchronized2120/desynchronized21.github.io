Among Us



Among us is the new sensation that has gripped the world of online gaming and streaming. The game is a really simple and compact game. Its main attracion is not its graphics or its gameplay but the simplicity of its concept and the loads of fun it has to offer to people. Group up with your crewmates and complete tasks and eliminate all the imposters or be an imposter and assassinate all the crewmates before the tasks are completed or you are ejected into the cold harsh space. The mobile version of the game is not too different from its PC counterpart and both the platforms can interact with each other. The game has a very simple concept, if you are a crewmate, you have to perform all the tasks before the imposter/s kills almost all the crewmates. If you are an Imposter, you have to kill all the crewmates before all the tasks are completed or before you are ejected into space.
